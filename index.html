About Us

Haba was founded with the mission of connecting mbogas with customers our team came together to build a better world through E-commerce we want to increase mboga sale with affordable price.

Haba is a platform that empowers communities to purchase fresh and healthy produce at lower prices by leveraging the power of group buying.Together, we can make wholesome food more affordable and accessible.
